File Converter is a privacy-first document conversion app that puts you in control of your data.

PRIVACY FIRST
All file conversion happens entirely on your device using WebAssembly-powered Pandoc. Your files never leave your phone. No uploads, no cloud processing, no data collection. We don't track you, and we can't access your files.

POWERFUL FORMAT SUPPORT
Convert between dozens of document formats:
• Markdown, HTML, DOCX (Word), EPUB, LaTeX, RTF, ODT
• ReStructuredText, AsciiDoc, Typst, Jupyter Notebooks
• JSON, CSV, and many more

Whether you're converting a Word document to Markdown, an EPUB to PDF, or a Jupyter notebook to HTML, File Converter handles it with precision.

WORKS OFFLINE
No internet connection required. All processing happens locally on your device, making it fast and reliable even without network access.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION
• Receive files from other apps via share intent
• Convert and share results to any app on your device
• Works with your favorite file managers and email clients

OPEN SOURCE & TRANSPARENT
The source code is publicly available for review. You can audit exactly what the app does with your files. No hidden behavior, no proprietary black boxes.

SIMPLE & INTUITIVE
Clean, straightforward interface. Select your input file, choose the output format, and convert. No complicated settings or confusing options.

Get started today and experience document conversion that respects your privacy.
